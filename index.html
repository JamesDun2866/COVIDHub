How to get your vaccine in the United Kingdom

In the United Kingdom ensure you Register with a GP when you are registered you will recieve a text message or a notification notifying you that you can sign up for a vaccine. What vaccine you get is dependant on supply but given at random unless you have a medical condition that prevents you taking one of the vacccines.


The United Kingdom Is currently in Lockdown

The United Kingdom has many restrictions currently in place and are now able to

- Send your Kids to School

- Meet with another person in a private space

There are plans to further release lockdown measures

March 29th you will be able to meet with up to 6 people and form a support bubble
April 12th you will be able to visit outdoor pubs and venues
17th May you will be able to visit pubs and venues indoors and expand you bubble up to 30 people
June 21st Lockdown will be lifted.

Please keep in mind these dates are subject to change so please keep an eye on local news.
